Covid-19 Info Hub

- the Covid-19 Info Hub aims to help users understand the risks of Covid-19 and make informed decisions in their day to day lives.

ABOUT THE PROJECT
Risk Free. Symptom spotter. Save lives.

Having hit by the coronavirus pandemic left lots of people vulnerable with conflicting information from all the mediums. There are so many resources out-there from news articles to social media that people tend to get overwhelm. It becomes harder for people to decipher through all the available information. Therefore, in order to make it efficient and resourceful I decided to help overcome this issue by creating an app called Covid-19 Info Hub.
The Covid-19 Info Hub aims to help users understand the risks of Covid-19 and make informed decisions in their day to day lives.

Platform: Android

Role: User Research, Prototyping, UI Design, UX Design, App Design

Tools: Figma, Miro, Lookback

The problem

Having been hit by the Covid-19 pandemic, conflicting information from many different sources has overwhelmed and misinformed people.

People need a reliable way to make informed decisions from trusted sources.

The research

I conducted interviews and surveys to understand how users got their information about Covid-19 and made day to day decisions during the pandemic.

Key insights we received were people needed help in processing information about which activities were safer than others.

From this, I developed a protytpe and performed usability testing which helped make a covid-19 risk questionaire simpler and easier to finish.

The solution

I have designed a mobile app to provide users the ability to see the prevalence of covid cases in their area, understand if

they should seek medical help if they show symptoms, learn the latest news about covid-19, and understand the relative risks associated with daily activities.

Covid-19 Screening Tool

One key insight was that people could not distinguish cold and flu symptoms from covid-19 symptoms.

As a result, I developed a screening tool to help a person decide to seek medical care.

Covid-19 Risk Tool

Another key insight is people could not process the amount of conflicting information about what activities were safer than others as they were coming out of lockdowns. I build a feature to allow users to search for an activity and understand the relative risk of that activity as compared to other common activities
