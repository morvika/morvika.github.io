Morvika Parmar

Product Designer


Hi, I’m Morvika. I have always been fond of designing and creating things for others. I am really passionate about human psychology and have an academic research background in psychology and cognitive science that helps me reflect on human thinking and behavior. My love for digital media and design have always been there as long as I remember.

I am really passionate when it comes to digital media, product design, graphic design and video editing and want to put my love for digital media and psychology to make the meaningful user experiences.

I live in the bay area and in my spare time, you can always find me enjoying the ocean breeze.

what I do
ux/ui design.

I focus on human-centered approach that often requires layers of research, prototyping, and testing. I also play close attention to all aspects of a product’s development, including design, usability, function, and branding.
I can make the entire end-to-end journey of a user’s interaction with a product.

ux research.

I gather data, and synthesizing that data in order to improve usability. I focus on learning about project requirements from stakeholders, and learning about the needs and goals of the end users. My research consists of interviews, surveys, observing prospects or current users, and review existing literature, data, usability tests or A/B tests.

web design.

I focus on all of the visual elements of a website, and can turn wireframe layouts into finished web page designs.
