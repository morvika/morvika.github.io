Meet App

Project Overview

Meet App helps people find and decide on local hangouts with their friends

Additional

Platform: Android

Role: User Research, Prototyping, UX Design

Tools: Figma, Lookback

The problem

There isn’t an easy way to figure what should you be doing especially when it comes to group of friends. It’s difficult and time consuming to figure out where to go and find new exciting things to do.

The research

The research was conducted through informal interviews which helped uncover what issues users are facing to plan hangouts and to learn how to help users find the way to plan an efficient hangout with their friends. From discussions with friends, I found that they would often be searching through yelp and looking for websites that listed the top things to do in the area and would often spend hours in planning this. On top of this, they would spend time in calling each place for availability for booking the activity. When a group was involved, this was more complicated, as sometimes the time the group decided wanted to do the activity was already booked out.

The solution

I have designed a mobile app to provide users the ability to see the prevalence of covid cases in their area, understand if

they should seek medical help if they show symptoms, learn the latest news about covid-19, and understand the relative risks associated with daily activities.

KEY INSIGHT

One key insight was that users spend a lot of time decide which category to explore and did not what is most applicable for them. Thus, I decided to scrap the category page to reduce cognitive load so they can see the results right away which makes it easier for them filter the categories. .

Polls

Another key insight is people could not process the amount of conflicting information about what activities were safer than others as they were coming out of lockdowns. I build a feature to allow users to search for an activity and understand the relative risk of that activity as compared to other common activities
