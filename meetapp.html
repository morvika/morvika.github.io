Outing Planner

Friends. Family. Get together.

Friend: Lets all hangout this weekend!
Me: Where?
Friends: Don't know yet?
Me: What time are you thinking?
Friends: Lets decide that later.

During the week we tried coming up with ideas but no one could agree on any. Friday is around the corner and we did not plan anything yet. Then on Friday night we start planing something and realize most of the things are booked. So out of frustration we decided to grab an ice cream in the neighbourhood. It wasn't exactly the weekend we were looking forward to.

I developed the Outing Planner App to keep our weekends lively and easy to plan!

The problem

When planning hangouts with friends or family, we often get confused and can't come up with a clear starting point. It turns out to be difficult and time consuming to decide. Usually we are left with questions such as    Where to go?      When to go?      Who should come?     How to get there?    In addition, finding we find it difficult to find new and exciting things to do.

The research

The research was conducted through informal interviews which helped uncover what issues users are facing to plan hangouts and to learn how to help users find the way to plan an efficient hangout with their friends. From discussions with friends, I found that they would often be searching through yelp and looking for websites that listed the top things to do in the area and would often spend hours in planning this.

On top of this, they would spend time in calling each place for availability for booking the activity. When a group was involved, this was more complicated and often by the time the group decides on an activity, it was already booked out.

The solution

In order to streamline this planning process, I designed a mobile app called the Outing Planner App. This mobile application helps people find and decide on local hangouts with their friends. The app also allows them come to an collective decision by using the polls feature and purchase the outing all in the app.

Insights

From usability testing, one key insight was that users spend a lot of time decide which category to explore. Thus, I decided to scrap the category page to reduce cognitive load so they can see the results right away. After seeing some results at the start, it was much easier for them to filter the categories.

Hangout Details

Accessing hangout is made really simple on the outing hangout. You are easily able to visulize the location of the hangout from the map. Hangouts can be searched through list view or map view. Hangout detail page displays variety of different shortcuts, which allows quick access to the website, phone number, messaging tool, location and reserveation price. Also allows to see other customer reviews and their personal images of the hangout site. Lastly it maps out direction details for a particular activity.

Polls

Deciding on which activity to choose is often difficult for a group. People often resort to creating polls in Messenger or other messaging apps. However, having to translate information about the activity to the poll is a tedious process. Also, polls created by users will often lack context on location, price and other information. Having the ability to create a poll directly within the app will allow friends to directly see more information about the activity to make a well informed decision.
