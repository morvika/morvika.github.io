Elegant Designs

Project Overview

The Elegant Spaces website helps users focus on the design which is most suitable for their home and directs them to the product which will be most ideal for their space. I have created a website which has various inspiration ideas and a wide collection of furniture to choose from. This will help narrow down choices and allow users to come to pick out their home furniture efficiently.

Platform: Web App

Role: User Research, Prototyping, UX/UI Design

Tools: Sketch, Lookback

The problem

My main goal was to make a website which provides design inspiration for a wide variety of home styles from Mid-Century Modern to Scandinavian. In addition to large furniture, I wanted include smaller pieces such as lightning and wall decor to help users make holistic design choices. I conducted user interviews and surveys to get a better understanding of the demands.

The research

Research started by my curiosity of buying furniture for my home. I was spending countless hours searching the Web and in store to find the perfect pieces that fit my home. It seemed very difficult to gather information so I decided to design a website that makes consumers' lives easier.

The solution

Using the insights, I have created a prototype for this website. A future area to explore will be integrating Augmented Reality to help users visualize designs in their homes.

Key Insights

During the research, I arrived at some key sights after conducting user interviews and surveys. 80% of the users like to see inspiring ideas and see how various furniture fit together. 70% of the users want to view product details directly from the inspirational design 60% of the users want to save their product choices to display later. 80% of users trust the product more if they see users reviews.

Design Concepts

I have implemented these insights in sketches and low fidelity design. I included more complete room designs in the website to help users how various furniture pieces fit together. The room design will include not only large furniture such as sofas and rugs, but also smaller components such as wall paintings and lighting fixtures.

For all components in the room design, the user can directly click the component to get more details and purchase rather than showing them a static photo where the products are not easily searchable. Also, the website will include save your favourite item to display later, this function will allow user to always refer back to their item on a later date.
